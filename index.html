Personal Project

Without this tracker I'd have had to check each company's jobs page by hand every day to stay on top of new openings — slow, and easy to miss things. So I built a tracker that automatically checks the government apprenticeship register three times a day for the banks and investment banks I'm targeting (HSBC, Barclays, Goldman Sachs, J.P. Morgan and more), filters out the roles that don't fit, and surfaces new relevant listings — with the role details — within hours of them going live. Instead of finding out days late, I can act on a new opening almost as soon as it appears. It's set up to send me a Telegram alert the moment the scraper spots one, so I don't even need to open the page to know. Built using HTML, JavaScript and PowerShell, with a lot of help from AI tools to bring it together given I'm not a developer by background.

04 · EPQ

My EPQ

To what extent will sectors change with the mass introduction of AI?

The most valuable people will be those who can ask good questions, spot weak answers, communicate clearly, adapt quickly and use human judgement. — Finance Director, primary survey 2026

This EPQ examines how far different sectors should change in response to AI, drawing on academic research, Goldman Sachs economic modelling, and a primary survey of 57 professionals across business, healthcare, law, education and finance. The core argument is that AI will reshape most sectors rather than simply replacing them, but the pace and nature of change will vary significantly depending on the role and how ready people are to adapt. The sectors facing the most urgent change are education, which is still built around memorisation, and the military, where autonomous weapons raise serious ethical questions that current international law has no answer for.

Primary research: I designed and ran an independent survey of 57 professionals across business, finance, healthcare, law, education and sales to build a real-world evidence base alongside the academic sources. More responses are being collected and results will be updated.
